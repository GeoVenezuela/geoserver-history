Description:
Abstract:
This dataset is a single polygon coverage representing the Central Region of Santa Fe County NM as defined by the Santa Fe County Population and Housing Study of August 1994. This Region encompasses the City of Santa Fe, Tesuque, Agua Fria, Eldorado, Las Campanas, and Arroyo Hondo. It is the population, employment, and geographic center of Santa Fe County. This area includes all 1990 U.S. Census Tracts in the City of Santa Fe and Census Tracts 2, 103.02, 103.03, 103.04, 104, and 105 of Santa Fe County. Most of Census Tract 102 of Santa Fe County is also included in this area.
Purpose:
This dataset provides a geographic subset of Santa Fe County for Population and Housing Analysis and Forcasting.
